# Incident Response

For any production incident, first declare it in the #incidents channel and assign an incident commander who coordinates the response. The incident commander does not fix things directly; they organize the people who do.

Severity levels: SEV1 means a full outage affecting all users, SEV2 means major degradation, and SEV3 means a minor issue with a workaround. SEV1 and SEV2 require a live war-room call.

Once the incident is resolved, the team must write a blameless postmortem within 48 hours. The postmortem focuses on system and process fixes, never on blaming individuals.
